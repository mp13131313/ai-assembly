## Architecture: why no pipeline change was needed

Orchestrator's transcription gate ([`overnight_orchestrator.transcription_state`](../../runtime/scripts/overnight_orchestrator.py:105)) reads per-session `status.json` with `state="done"`. Researcher reads `session_package.json` via `*/session_package.json` glob. The two files at the right path are sufficient — vendor sessions are indistinguishable downstream. Walking-session reflections already follow the same pattern (vendor JSON, no audio).

So C24 is "land two files at the boundary," not "build a parallel pipeline."
