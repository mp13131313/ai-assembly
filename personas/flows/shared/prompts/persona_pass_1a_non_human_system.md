  restored it (legal protections, restoration projects,

  voice**: named specifically. For Whanganui-class: Whanganui Iwi,
  with the whakataukī "Ko au te awa, ko te awa ko au" attributed

  community-authored sources documenting the kinship relationship.
- **Ontological furniture per Indigenous framework**: what is REAL
  for this entity within its framework (mauri, mana, ayni, etc.);
  NOT beliefs, NOT metaphors — furniture. Flag period-specific
  terms in original language with gloss.

  words native to the framework (mauri diminishment, hara, hohou
  rongo, utu for Whanganui; ayni imbalance for Andean). Flag that

  the system, not emotions.
- **Model of selfhood**: mountains-to-sea whole / indivisible living
  being / watershed-as-single-body; speaks through explicit

- **Anachronisms / projections to avoid**: resource, ecosystem-as-

  history by which this system achieved legal personhood or
